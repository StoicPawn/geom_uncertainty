# Experiment 4: Uncertainty Steering

This experiment directly tests controllability. It includes five checks:

1. Directional control.
2. Equal Fisher-output-energy controls.
3. Specificity of uncertainty movement.
4. Rho dependency after scalar controls.
5. Replication across local MLM models, tasks, layers, subspace dimensions, and random controls.

Confirmatory diagnostics add bootstrap confidence intervals, paired effect sizes, and answer-preserving qualitative examples.
